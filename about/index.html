Eric Brock is an M.Arch III candidate at The Design School, Arizona State University. He completed his first year of graduate study at Catholic University of America, where coursework focused on classical architecture and technical drawing.

Before graduate school, Eric served as an admissions officer at Johns Hopkins University and Howard University, where he worked directly with prospective students and their families across thousands of applications.

Eric is interested in pursuing an architectural design career where the work is material and the impact is direct.
