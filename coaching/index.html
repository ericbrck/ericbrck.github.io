In between studio and site visits, I coach students on college applications. Mostly summers, some during the school year. The work is figuring out what's actually there in a student's story, then helping them say it clearly.

As a first-gen student, I applied to around 40 schools without access to a counselor or consultant. What it takes to get in shouldn't be hidden behind a desk at some elite university.

I know how frustrating this process is for families navigating it for the first time. I've sat on both sides of that desk, as a student without a roadmap and as an admissions officer at two universities. My goal is to demystify it and help students directly.
